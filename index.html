How It Works

The TQ Cup is a simulated tournament running in Football Manager. Each participant may acquire the right to support a specific national team by holding the $TQ token.

Matches are automatically simulated by the game's engine — there is no human intervention. Team performance results from in-game data and player attributes.

This is the first competition. Soon we will run additional cups such as a traditional World Cup, Champions League formats, and more.

How to Participate

To participate, deposit 3,000,000 $TQ tokens into the tournament's official public wallet. After the deposit, comment on the token's page indicating which national team you chose to support.

About the Game

Football Manager is one of the most advanced football management simulators. Developed by Sports Interactive, the game places the user in the role of manager, handling tactics, transfers, finances and player development.

Matches are decided by the simulation engine using realistic player stats and team data — there is no human manipulation of match outcomes in this tournament.
