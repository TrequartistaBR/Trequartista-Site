How It Works

The TQ Cup is a simulated tournament run inside Football Manager. Each participant can back one national team by purchasing the right with $TQ tokens.

All matches are simulated by the game's engine — there is no human interference. Results depend on in-game player data and team attributes.

How to Participate

1) Transfer the participation amount to the tournament's official public wallet (see below). 2) After the on-chain confirmation, leave a comment on the token page stating which national team you back.

Prizes

Of the total collected, 18% of tokens will be burned — contributing to the appreciation of the $TQ ecosystem.

The remaining 82% will be distributed cumulatively among the teams according to their performance.

Round of 16

Teams that reach the Round of 16 receive 1%.

1%

Quarterfinals

Teams that reach the Quarterfinals receive an additional 2% (cumulative 3%).

3%

Semifinals

Semifinalists receive an additional 5% (cumulative 8%).

8%

Runner-up

The runner-up receives an additional 10% (cumulative 18%).

18%

Champion

The champion receives an additional 20% (cumulative 28%).

28%
